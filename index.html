Day 580. Still here. Still fighting.

One Step Collective started with a single question: what if the most honest thing you could do was just show up — broken, tired, and trying anyway?

Started by Justin, this is a space for people who know what it's like to hit the floor — and who are still deciding to get back up. Not because it's easy. Because it's the only move left.

Recovery isn't a straight line. It's a series of rounds. Some you win. Some you survive. All of them count.

This community is for the survivors, the seekers, and the ones still mid-fall. You belong here.
